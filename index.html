About Me

I was born and raised in Mongolia, where I experienced a true nomadic lifestyle before journeying out into the world. Over the years, I’ve traveled to more than 30 countries and lived in Germany and Singapore for professional commitments.

I’m passionate about creativity, and the arts, including films, fashion, and design. I wish to bring ideas to life making a positive impact on people’s lives. My greatest hobby is to find peace in simple things — walking near the ocean or wandering quietly through a forest.

At times, I can be a little too philosophical — perhaps an old soul lost in thought — but that’s part of who I am.
